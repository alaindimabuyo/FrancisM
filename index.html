Francis Magalona

Father of Pinoy Hip-Hop

1964-2009

Francis Magalona was born on October 4, 1964 in Manila, Philippines as Francis Michael Durango Magalona. He was an actor, known for Kung aagawin mo ang lahat sa akin (1987), Engkanto (1992) and Txt (2006).

Tagged as the Master Rapper of the Philippines. He was the first Philippine-based Filipino to record a rap album, Yo!, in 1989 that produced his signature hit, Mga Kababayan Ko!.
